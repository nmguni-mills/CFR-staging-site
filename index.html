About

Contemporary Furniture Rentals Inc. is Canada’s largest event furniture rental company. The depth and breadth of inventory includes the largest selection of colours, finishes, and sizes from which to design your event. Our custom made Canadian collection, specifically designed for events includes more then twelve colours in upholstered goods, and more the five colours and textures for bars, food stations, dining tables, communal, occasional tables, and other case goods. We also offer North America’s largest collection of Philippe Starck designed Ghost chairs, barstools, and accent pieces which can be seen in person in our, by appointment only, showroom. Developing trends for event planners and designers is always on our minds so please contact us with any ideas or requests you may have so that we can help you make your event s all that they need to be. Please contact us with any questions, requests or ideas you have for your great events.
